About Hackathon


A hackathon is a competitive event in which a team of designers, developers, and subject matter experts collaborate to create solutions for a specific problem within a defined time frame. The goal is to build a working prototype, in the form of a website, an app, or a robot, to solve a given problem.


Also known as a “Hackfest” or a “Hack day,” the event gives participants the opportunity to network, collaborate, and productize their ideas.


Vidyalankar Polytechnic's Hackathon is a Hackathon at smaller level to provide students an idea about Software and Hadware Hackathons conducted at national and state level, it will also provide a platform to solve some of the problems we face in our daily lives, and thus inculcate a culture of product innovation and a mindset of problem solving.


Mini Hackathon will be conducted in offline mode where team has to build a solution for the given problem statements provided from industries, in the form of a website within a time frame of 16 hours.


How to form a team for Hackathon?

  • Each team would comprise of 6 members including the team leader.
  • It is compulsory to have atleast a girl member in a Team.
  • Repetition of member in 2 different teams is not allowed.
  • Each team has to select one faculty as their team mentor who will be with team through out the competition.
  • Once team have registered, changes in team members or mentor will not be encouraged.
